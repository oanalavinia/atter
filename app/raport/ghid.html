Abstract

Atter is an application designed for students of Computer Science Faculty of Iasi that has as a purpose the implementation of a system that manages the courses and seminars. Users are of two types, teachers that can register a seminar/course, add marks and visualise different reports, and the students that can attend a seminar/course and also see some informations provided by the teacher.

First page

Login

The login is made based on the fenrir account provided by the faculty and by typing the email and password you are redirected to the interface specific of you status, student or professor.

Attend/Register class

The register component is available for students and professors, but with different functionalities. The way it monitors the attendance at the seminars is embedded around the fact that the teacher receives the code associated with that week when he / she generates a course. By providing this code to the students it is assured that only the present can have access to it. But as additional measures the application checkes the location of each student wishing to attend a seminar so that he can not do it if he / she is outside the faculty. It is also not allowed to register after the seminar hours.

Attend class

As a student, you can participate in a class by specifying the code provided by the teacher at the seminar and his / her email address. The presence will be automatically recorded so that it can be after viewed on a different page.

Register class

Completing the form on this page it will automatically be registered a week and also generated a code on which students can be identified as present at that seminar when they type it in the application.

Check courses

The management of the information that is seen about a course is also changed depending on the needs that each type of user may have.

Student

For each course you have been assigned to, you can see all the weeks the teacher has registered and the status you have for them, the points and the presence.

Professor

As a professor you have 2 categories of items in Check module of the menu: course and laboratory.

Course

If you are the course professor of a subject there are 2 charts that are showing the general points an attendance of each group.

Seminar

For each lab you can see the groups you are assigned to, divided by weeks, with a separate page that brings present students in real time. Also on this page you have the possibility to mark the students.

In addition to the information on attendance for the seminar, there are also 2 diagrams focused on these groups.
