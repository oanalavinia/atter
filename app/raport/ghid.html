Abstract

Atter is an application designed for students of Computer Science Faculty of Iasi that has as a purpose the implementation of a system that manages the courses and seminars. Users are of two types, teachers that can register a seminar/course, add marks, notes for students and visualise different reports, and the students that can attend a seminar/course and also see some informations provided by the teacher.

First page

Login

The login is made based on the fenrir account provided by the faculty and by typing the email and password you are redirected to the interface specific of you status, student or professor.

Attend/Register class

The register component is available for students and professors, but with different functionalities.

Attend class

As a student you can attend a class by specifying the code provided by the teacher at the seminar. Also, the location is gonna be checked so you cannot attend a seminar without being in the area of the faculty.

Register class

When registering a course, you'll receive an unique code that should be given to the students so they can register.

Check courses

The management of the information that is seen about a course is also changed depending on the needs that each type of user may have.

Student

For each course that you are assign to, you may see all the weeks that are registered by the professor and the status you have on each one, points and attendance.

Professor

As a professor you have 2 categories of items in Check module of the menu: course and laboratory.

Course

If you are the course professor of a subject there are 2 charts that are showing the general points an attendance of each group.

Lab

For each lab you can see the groups you are assigned, divided by weeks, with a separate page that brings in real-time students. It also provides functionality for giving points.

Besides attending the seminar, there are also 2 charts specific to the groups of that subject.
